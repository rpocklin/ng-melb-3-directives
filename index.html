    • Directives - why are they good?

  • because modular code is important
  • you can avoid re-inventing wheels ...
  • share knowledge and give-back to the community ...




    • Directives Headaches

  • Directives in Angular.js can be hard
  • but they can save you a heap of time ...

  • writing tests for directives can also be hard
  • but then you can safely change your code ...

  • extracting directives from your codebase can be tricky
  • but then you can release and maintain it on Github...
  • First Directive - Timeline

  • Directive type: View Template
  • Motivation: To build an intuitive timeline hierarchy
    Rather than: several divs with classes (yuck!)
